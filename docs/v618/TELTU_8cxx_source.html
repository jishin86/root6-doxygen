17 /** \class TELTU
18 \ingroup g3d
19 A cylinder with an elliptical section. It has three
20 parameters: the ellipse semi-axis in X, the ellipse
21 semi-axis in Y and the half length in Z. The equation of
22 the conical curve is:
23 
24  X**2/fRx**2 + Y**2/fRy**2 = 1
25 
26 ELTU is not divisible.
27 
28  - name: name of the shape
29  - title: shape's title
30  - material: (see TMaterial)
31  - rx: the ellipse semi-axis in X
32  - ry: the ellipse semi-axis in Y
33  - dz: half-length in z
34 */
